## Overview

Cine Rent West is the Pacific Northwest's go-to film and camera rental house, the studio behind a steady stream of commercial, music video, and documentary productions in Portland. I lead social media for CRW as their Social Media Director, owning the channel from strategy through execution.

![Two photographers shooting the race-liveried Ducati under the overhead softbox at Cine Rent West](/images/editorial/crw-bike-02.jpg)

## Role

This is a lead content role, not a camera for hire. I set the content direction, decide what gets captured as productions move through the studio, direct and shoot the behind-the-scenes coverage, edit it, and run the posting cadence that keeps the studio's channels active between shoots. Strategy, capture, edit, and delivery, all of it.

## Wins

I've run Cine Rent West's social channels for three years. A few wins along the way: a single reel, *"Is the M18 Obsolete?"*, broke out to 55,105 views and 340 new followers. In one quarter, reach jumped +642%, almost all of it organic. And across the account, 87.6% of views come from people who don't follow it yet, which is how the channel keeps finding its next audience.
