## Overview

*Make It Here* is a short film I made with Oregon Film, the state's official film office and support body for the Oregon film industry. I developed and led it in partnership with Cine Rent West and North East Productions, working as producer, DP, and co-editor.

![Behind the scenes on Make It Here, collaborating at the camera](/images/editorial/make-it-here-01.jpg)

## Role

I handled the full arc of production: conceiving and pitching the project, leading the camera department on set, and co-editing the final cut.

![On-set interview setup for Make It Here, crew and green screen behind](/images/editorial/make-it-here-02.jpg)

## Exhibition

Featured on Oregon Film's official channels and selected for screening at the Portland Airport Theater, a curated venue dedicated to showcasing Oregon-made film.

![Interview subject on a white-cyc stage for Make It Here](/images/editorial/make-it-here-03.jpg)
