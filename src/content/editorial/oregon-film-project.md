## Overview

A short film produced in partnership with Oregon Film — the state's official film office and support body for the Oregon film industry. The project originated as a pitch developed through a Portland studio partnership, with Martin driving the project as DP, co-editor, and producer.

## Role

Martin handled the full arc of production: conceiving and pitching the project, leading the camera department on set, and co-editing the final cut.

## Exhibition

Featured on Oregon Film's official channels and selected for screening at the Portland Airport Theater — a curated venue dedicated to showcasing Oregon-made film.
