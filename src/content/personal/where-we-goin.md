## The Film

*Where We Goin* is a 30-minute documentary and series pilot directed by Devin Boss. The film earned awards on the festival circuit and has been featured in screenings and programming across the region.

## Role

Martin served as Director of Photography — building the visual language for the film in collaboration with Devin Boss's direction.
