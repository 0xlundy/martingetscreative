## The Film

*Against the Code* is a feature documentary about the world of indie game development: the people who build games outside the mainstream, the culture they've built around it, and what it costs to keep going.

Production is ongoing.

![On set for Against the Code, two subjects reviewing papers on a brightly lit table in a dark studio](/images/stories/DSCF7297.jpg)

## Role

I'm the lead creative producer. On an independent doc that means wearing a lot of hats: I help drive the fundraising, run production, shoot in the field, and supervise post. It's a real collaboration with a team, and I own a meaningful part of it, from the grant applications through the final cut.

## Support

The film is grant funded. It's backed by a Precipice Fund grant, supported by the **Andy Warhol Foundation**, plus a grant from the **Regional Arts & Culture Council (RACC)**.
